Our goal


Our ultimate goal is to empower, develop and strenghten the skills and abilities of individuals and communities to effectively address challenges, make informed decisions and achieve their set goals and objectives and this can be achieved through capacity building, training and mentoring.

Our Vision


To be the Oasis for the lost hope upon which their seeds of survival and greatness will grow and flourish by creating a world where every individual and community has access to basic necessities of life, opportunities, resources and support needed to reach their full potential

Our Mission


To continuously reduce the garment of unactualized dreams buried in our cemetery by empowering and mentoring individuals and communities through capacity building initiatives, fostering self-resilience, self-reliance and sustainable growth,
